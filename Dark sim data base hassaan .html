System Usage Guide
Pakistan SIM: Enter mobile number starting with 03 (without country code) or full CNIC without dashes. Press TRACE NODE.
Indian Info: Enter 10-digit Indian mobile number without country code (no +91). Press SEARCH INFO.
Themes: Tap sun icon (top right) to switch between 7 themes. Toggle dark/light mode from same panel.
Effects: Click the sliders button (bottom right) to toggle Rain, Glitch, and Particle effects on/off.
Music: Use the player at the bottom to play/pause, skip tracks. Music plays in background automatically.
